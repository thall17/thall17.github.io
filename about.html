About Me

Hi! I'm Tim, a Quality Engineer at Apple, software/web developer, and graduate student based in Los Angeles, California. I love creating innovative applications with intuitive designs that can make a positive impact. I'm actively looking for new software opportunities in the Bay Area with a plan to move in early 2018. I'm most experienced in web development - specifically Ruby on Rails - but have worked with many different frameworks and languages, and am always excited to leverage my diverse professional and educational background to develop new skills.

From there, I've advanced in a career in the tech sector, learning through a combination of independent study, on-the-job experience, and formal education. The MOOC model has allowed me to build a strong foundation in computer science, gain certificates in Ruby on Rails, Python programming, and machine learning from accredited universities, and currently I'm on track to complete the Georgia Tech OMSCS program (MS, Computer Science) degree - specializing in Artificial/Interactive Intelligence and Machine Learning - in 2019. My long-term goal is to complement my growing skill set in software engineering with the statistical analysis and societal investigation skills I gained at UCLA.
